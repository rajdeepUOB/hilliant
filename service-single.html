FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Data engineering ensures efficient data processing, storage, and retrieval for seamless decision-making and analytics.

What does a data engineer do?

A data engineer designs, builds, and maintains data pipelines, ensuring data is collected, transformed, and stored efficiently for analytics and business intelligence.

What technologies are used in data engineering?

Data engineers commonly work with tools like Apache Spark, Hadoop, Kafka, Airflow, SQL, Python, and cloud platforms such as AWS, Azure, and Google Cloud.

How does data engineering support machine learning?

Data engineering provides clean, structured, and scalable datasets that machine learning models rely on for accurate training, testing, and deployment.

What are the costs associated with data engineering solutions?

Costs depend on infrastructure, cloud usage, and data processing needs. Cloud-based data engineering solutions can vary from pay-as-you-go pricing to fixed enterprise packages.
